# Policy And FAQ Handbook

This file contains a draft knowledge pack for TechXchange chatbot ingestion.
Content is written as an operational draft for MVP and should be reviewed by business and legal owners before public launch.

## Policy Set

### 1. Warranty Policy
- TechXchange supports warranty handling based on the seller commitment, manufacturer warranty, and the product condition shown at checkout.
- Brand new products follow the official manufacturer or authorized distributor warranty when applicable.
- Used products follow the warranty period committed by the store or seller on the product page.
- Customers should keep the order code, invoice, serial number, and all bundled accessories for warranty verification.
- Warranty does not apply to products damaged by water, impact, fire, misuse, unauthorized repair, or missing serial information.
- Standard warranty intake time is 1 to 3 working days. Resolution time depends on the seller, brand service center, or replacement stock availability.

### 2. Return And Refund Policy
- Customers may request return or refund when the delivered product is damaged in transit, not as described, missing key accessories, or has a manufacturer fault within the allowed period.
- Return requests should be submitted within 3 days after delivery for shipping damage or missing items, and within 7 days for hidden technical faults unless the product page states otherwise.
- Returned products must include the main unit, bundled accessories, packaging if still available, and proof of purchase.
- Refunds are processed after inspection confirms the issue is valid and not caused by user misuse.
- If the issue can be resolved by replacement, repair, or missing accessory compensation, TechXchange may propose that option before refund.
- Refund processing target is 3 to 7 working days after approval, depending on the payment channel.

### 3. Payment Policy
- TechXchange supports payment methods configured by each store, including bank transfer, online payment gateway, and cash on delivery where available.
- Orders are confirmed only after payment is successfully recorded or the seller accepts a valid cash on delivery request.
- For prepaid orders, customers should transfer the exact amount using the correct order code to avoid delayed confirmation.
- Failed or duplicated payments are reviewed manually when needed, and refunds are processed after reconciliation.
- Promotional discounts, shipping subsidies, and vouchers are applied only when all eligibility conditions are met at checkout time.

### 4. Shipping And Delivery Policy
- Delivery time depends on the store location, carrier capacity, destination province, and product readiness.
- Ready to ship items are normally handed to the carrier within 1 to 2 working days after confirmation.
- Multi-item orders may be split when products come from different stores or warehouses.
- Customers should inspect the package condition when receiving the order and report visible damage immediately.
- Shipping fees, delivery estimates, and supported regions are displayed during checkout based on the selected address.

### 5. Cancellation Policy
- Customers may cancel an order before the seller confirms packing or handoff to the carrier.
- Orders that are already packed, shipped, or custom-prepared may not be eligible for direct cancellation and may need to follow the return process instead.
- TechXchange may cancel orders with failed payment verification, suspicious activity, out-of-stock items, or invalid pricing caused by a system error.
- If a prepaid order is cancelled by the system or seller, the refund will follow the normal refund timeline.

### 6. Product Condition And Authenticity Policy
- Each listing should clearly state whether the product is new, like new, used, refurbished, or open box when relevant.
- Product condition percentage, warranty period, included accessories, and defects should be disclosed on the product page.
- Sellers are responsible for listing accuracy, authenticity, and availability of the product at the moment of order acceptance.
- TechXchange reserves the right to remove listings that appear misleading, counterfeit, duplicated, or unsafe.

## FAQ Set

### 1. How do I check warranty eligibility?
- Open the order detail and product detail page to review the seller warranty commitment, warranty duration, and serial or invoice requirements.
- If needed, contact support with the order code and product serial number for verification.

### 2. When can I request a return?
- You can request a return when the product is damaged on arrival, not as described, missing important accessories, or has a verified technical fault within the allowed time window.

### 3. How long does a refund take?
- Approved refunds usually take 3 to 7 working days after inspection and approval.
- Bank processing time can vary depending on the payment method.

### 4. What payment methods are supported?
- Supported payment methods depend on the store and can include bank transfer, online payment, and cash on delivery where available.

### 5. Why is my order not confirmed yet?
- Common reasons include pending bank reconciliation, incorrect transfer content, seller review delay, or stock verification.

### 6. Can I inspect the product before paying?
- Inspection availability depends on the shipping method and store policy.
- Customers should check the product page and checkout notes for the exact support level.

### 7. What happens if the package arrives damaged?
- Take photos of the package, do not discard the packaging, and report the issue to support as soon as possible with the order code.

### 8. Are used products tested before sale?
- Used products should be tested and described by the seller before listing.
- The exact testing scope may differ by store and product type, so customers should review the listing carefully.

### 9. Can I cancel an order after payment?
- If the order has not been packed or handed to the carrier, cancellation is usually possible.
- If it is already being shipped, the return flow may apply instead.

### 10. How do I report a product that is not as described?
- Use the support channel with the order code, photos, videos if available, and a short description of the mismatch.
